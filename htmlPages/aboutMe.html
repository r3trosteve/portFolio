Hi, my name is Rosalind Cervante and I'm a web designer based in Wellington, New Zealand.

I have studied graphic design for one year at a Dutch art college called Koninklijke Academie van Beeldende Kunsten (KABK), and upon returning to New Zealand picked up graphic design at Massey Wellington for another year, after doing a course in Python while there, I left Massey for Yoobee School of Design to focus on web based design. I am currently studying web design at Yoobee, and will graduate December 2017.

I am now hoping to focus on web design and web development for a career, and continue with drawing and art in spare time. My current strengths are more on the design side of programming but am looking to broaden my tech skills with work experience over next few years.
