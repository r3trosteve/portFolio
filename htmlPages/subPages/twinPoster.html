This was an assignment for my typography class, each student had to select a typographical poster they admired, I selected the smaller image, author unknown, but was found on a Polish website. then we had to recreate it A1, using all the same elements, a 'fraternal twin, the cute one' our tutor told us. The poster was made by hand using paper cutouts, at the end the all the paper and glue took over the image so I took a high quality photo of it and took that opportunity to put some dots over it in photoshop, then printed it.

Made in 2015.
