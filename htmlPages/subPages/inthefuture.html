This is a poster about the future and the uncertainty of the lifespan of the Earth, I try to include the reference to global warming with the 2 sides being different temperatures but the confusing text over the top, each line contradicting the next meaning we don't really know what's going to happen and even if global warming is an issue, we'll probably run out of food and even more likely water beforehand and become extinct that way. the song is also really good, it was written by David Byrne of Talking Heads, unfortunately the song wasn't long enough for the font I wanted to use, so the last few lines are mine, so sorry David.

Made in 2015 alongside Manon van Rookhuijzen.
