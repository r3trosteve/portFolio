This is my insomnia series, when I was jet lagged one time I realised there was 3 stages I went through, one was being extremely bored, followed by thinking crazy things and then feelings of sleepiness would come and go. So for this I set my camera to a thirty second shutter speed and shined 3 different coloured lights on my model, each representing the different stages, green - bored, red - crazy and blue is sleepy.

Made in 2015, model: Lili Servante.
