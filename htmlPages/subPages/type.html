This was dont in typography class, we had to get checked paper and then make our own typography using only squares and right angled triangles, then trace over them and finally pick one letter from one of your fonts and finalise it. On the right is a collection of the fonts, on the checked paper and traced over, then I focussed on the 'r' for Rosalind. The final on the left is riso-printed and looks very different from the original and even the one half way through the process.

Made in 2015.
