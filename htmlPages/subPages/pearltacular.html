To get into the KABK we had a project to create our alter ego, since another assignment to get in was to pick something we were passionate about and make a poster about that (for which I picked shark-finning) I decided to make ocean-related-things a theme of my entrance exam. So I made this superhero mermaid called 'Pearltacular', we were only allowed to use two colors, so she's what was meant to be my skin-colour with my favourite colour, pale green. After designing the person I then decided to turn the poster into a comic book cover as it fit the 'superhero' idea, and comic books are awesome.

Made in 2014.
