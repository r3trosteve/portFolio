This was just an assignment from the KABK in Holland that we students did after we returned from Czech and the big annual graphic design festival there. We had to pick a theme, make a font inspired by what we saw, then silkscreen a poster using our font that applies to our theme. I picked crafting as my theme, which is making things by hand, I picked this one for my final poster despite the strange patterns the ink made, especially bottom right corner as I thought it applied to my theme.

Made in 2015.
