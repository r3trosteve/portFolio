I'm a real big animal lover, with special connection to sharks. A 2015 KABK assignment was to create posters about something we are passionate about - I created anti shark-finning posters. I expanded on that a year later when we had an identical assignment at Massey, I made clay sharks and fins and photographed them on a beach, in a cooking pot and in a baby bell packaging net.

Made in 2016.
