As a child I became really obsessed with sharks after watching 'Deep Blue Sea', to this day shark movies are the one type of movie that really scare me. I first heard of shark finning after reading about 'Fight Club' and therefore Edward Norton, who is a figure against finning sharks. Now I'm still really interested in shark welfare, not only is it cruel but we are killing way too many for it to be sustainable, so whenever I have an assigment about something I care about I expand on shark finning. The first ones I did were the cartoony ones for the KABK entrance, then at Massey over a year later I made a series of three photographs of the clay sculptures I made.

Made in 2014 and 2016.
